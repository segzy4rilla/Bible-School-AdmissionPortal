1. How do I convert a picture of my document to a PDF?

How Clear Scanner works

When you open the app you'll have two options:
1. To take a picture of the document you want to scan
2. To select an already existing photo

You then scan, choose the format you want it to be saved as (preferably PDF).
If the document you are scanning has two pages or more make sure that you scan each page. Before you save, click on the camera button for another page. You will have two scanned documents.
Choose PDF and automatically those pages will be attached to make one document.

5. Tips

- A laptop/computer gives the best view of the website

- Use the latest upgraded internet browser (chrome is recommended)

- Clear Cache (google instructions on how to clear cache)

- Scroll to the bottom of every page

- Fill in every field and write Not Applicable if it doesn’t apply to you

- Avoid using symbols such as + or ‘ or -

- Convert pictures of documents to pdf before you upload
